56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races Mens 5000 Meters D1 Boys

Sat Sep 14 2024

Ann Arbor Skyline Results

The Skyline men's cross country team finished 12th with a total score of 309. Leading the team was Nicholas Yuan who posted a time of 17:31.6 followed closely by Jack Robichaud (17:43.1) and Enshu Kuan (17:44.4). Bruno Cifaldi (17:57.2) and Kyle Krasan (18:01.8) rounded out the top five showcasing consistent performances across the board. The team demonstrated strong efforts in a competitive field keeping a tight spread among the top runners and building momentum for the rest of the season.
