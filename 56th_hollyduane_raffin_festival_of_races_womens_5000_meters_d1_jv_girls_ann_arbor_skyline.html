56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races Womens 5000 Meters D1 JV Girls

Sat Sep 14 2024

Ann Arbor Skyline Results

In the JV girls' race Ann Arbor Skyline secured third place overall with 64 points. Standout performances included Gwen Stotts-Walshe who finished with a time of 23:00.7 and Tia Cocciolone who crossed the line at 23:10.6. Other notable finishes for Skyline were Elin Tenbrink at 23:19.4 Adrienne Stewart at 23:31.1 and Violet Olley with a personal record of 23:46.1. Their collective effort helped Skyline maintain a competitive edge in the race.
