56th Holly-Duane Raffin Festival of Races Mens 5000 Meters D1 JV Boys

Sat Sep 14 2024

Ann Arbor Skyline Results

In the JV boys' race the Skyline team placed 3rd with 72 points led by Oskar MacArthur (17:57.7) and Martin Gehrke (17:58.8) both setting personal records. Strong performances also came from Dylan Hanley (18:13.9) Parker Razelun (18:21.8) and Santiago Valenzuela (18:56.7) all contributing to the team's successful finish. Skyline maintained a close spread between its top runners highlighting their depth and consistency as they continue to build momentum in the season.
